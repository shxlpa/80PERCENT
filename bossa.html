Bossa is a design studio driven by first-principles. We bring professional hardware & software experience to our creative practice. Bossa has focused on audio projects, album releases, web design, low power skiing equipment, and art installations. We can bring bold ideas and forms to life, whether electromechanical, spatial, or acoustic in nature. The team consists of Shilpa and her diverse network of artists, mechanical/software/electrical engineers, wood and metalworkers, etc.

Philosophy
The best work happens at the intersection of engineering rigor and creative instinct. I don't separate the two.

BOSSA is intentionally small. I take on a handful of projects at a time, each one chosen because it's interesting and because I can bring something unique to it. Every engagement is a collaboration — I work closely with clients to understand not just what they want, but why they want it. The result is work that feels personal, precise, and built to last.
